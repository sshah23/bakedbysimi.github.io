About Simi

Baked by Simi is a home bakery based in Isleworth, West London, specialising in colourful, buttercream celebration cakes. Every cake is made to order, from scratch, using quality ingredients and plenty of love.

Whether you already have a clear design in mind or just a theme and a date, Simi will help you bring it to life.
